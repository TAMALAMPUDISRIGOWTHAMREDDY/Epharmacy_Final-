First Aid for Electric Shocks

Electric Shock First Aid Video:



Steps to Follow:

  1. Turn Off the Power: If safe, disconnect the power source.
  2. Do Not Touch the Victim: Use a non-conductive object like a wooden stick to move them away.
  3. Check for Breathing and Pulse: If the person is not breathing, begin CPR.
  4. Treat Burns: Cover burns with a clean, non-stick dressing.
  5. Keep the Person Warm: Cover them with a blanket to prevent shock.
  6. Seek Emergency Help: Call for medical assistance immediately.
